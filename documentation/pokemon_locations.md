# Pokémon Locations


## Static encounters & trade/gifts

### Static encounters

| Location | Pokémon | Level | Notes |
| :--- | --- | --- | --- |
| Power Plant | Zapdos | 50 | Legendary encounter. |
| Pokémon Tower 6F | Ghost Marowak | 30 | Requires Silph Scope to identify/battle. |
| Vermilion Dock | Mew | 65 | Revealed by using Strength to push the truck. |
| Seafoam Islands | Articuno | 50 | Legendary encounter. |
| Victory Road 1F | Moltres | 50 | Legendary encounter. |
| Cinnabar Volcano | Moltres | 70 | Alternative encounter in volcano area. |
| Cerulean Cave B1F | Mewtwo | 70 | Legendary encounter. |

If you catch 50 Pokémon, you can unlock the Pokédex’s **Wild Distribution Map** by speaking to Oak’s Aide in Fuchsia City’s meeting room. This mode allows you to view wild Pokémon encounter rates by area on the Town Map, including per-floor breakdowns for multi-floor dungeons:

![town map wild data](../screenshots/town_map_wild_data.png?raw=true)

For a preview of this data, see: [data/wild](../data/wild)
